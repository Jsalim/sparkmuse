What You Will Find

I've tried to bring the essence of Sparkmuse to form in this alpha. The discussion pages are where I've spent the most effort trying to make a useful tool to document, discuss, and collaborate on your ideas. You will stumble upon a few unimplemented features, several noticable omissions (where are you pagination!), and probably a bug or two. My goal was to get the concept in front of you sooner rather than later and learn how to shape the community and site to empower your ambitions!

Getting Started

  1. If you haven't done so already, check out our About page to read up on the general concept.
  2. Check out the Guidelines page to get a feeling for the form of discussion on Sparkmuse.
  3. Please fill out your bio on your Profile page: #{nameTag user: currentUser /}. Sparkmuse isn't just about discussing your ideas, it's about connecting you with others who share your interests. Your bio is an opportunity for you to open the doors to these connections.
  4. Check out our Sparks, let people know your opinion! You will find a variety of posting options on each Spark that will help you contribute to the discussion.
