How It Works

Do you want a problem solved? Do you wish an app existed? Convince a community of entrepreneurs to build it just by telling them what you wish you had.

Have Problems?

Lets take Sue, a sweet lady recently diagnosed with diabetes. The doctor gives her the bad news, some advice, and sends on her on her way. She gets home and looks in her cupboard in paralyzing fear, what can she eat! It's a new world for her and everything she knew about food has been turned upside down.

Sue starts looking up information on the internet. General guides on what to avoid, specifics about things in her pantry, and groups that can give her advice. There has got to be a better way! She mozies over to Google to pop the big question and Google spends all of 0.00001 seconds to say, "No, there is no better way."

Sue's story isn't unique, she has one of the thousands of problems that modern tech hasn't helped solve yet. But now she isn't powerless to do anything. Come to The Spark Foundry and tell us what ails you!

Let Us Know

All you have to do is give us a brief description of your problem and why you think it would be valuable to solve. You can ask for anything your heart desires: information services, mobile apps, productivity tools, or even games. We'll present your wish to our community of entrepreneurs giving them an exciting new idea to work on.

Convince Them To Work For You

Voting for an idea is a promise of attention. The biggest risk for an entrepreneur is that no one will care about the product or service they are building. We change that by using your email address as currency. The more votes an idea gets, the more attention it will bring to our community. So share your wish with everyone you can and convince them to take up the cause. The power is in your hands to get problems solved and help build exciting new businesses.

When the pot gets attractive enough, you can be sure there will be plenty of buzz. Entrepreneurs will debate how to best solve your need and work together to build it. Once they have built a working prototype, we will give them the opportunity to send you an email to sell you on what they've built. But don't worry, we will never sell or give away your email!
