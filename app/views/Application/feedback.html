About Sparkmuse

Sparkmuse is the beginning of a community in which I've always wanted to participate. "That's a cool idea" has crossed my mind so many times that I've yearned for a place to discuss, vet, and organize those thoughts. Over the years of reading Hacker News, there have often been "Rate My Idea" posts and some who outright said they wish they had a more formal place to share them. Sparkmuse will hopefully become that place but it can only succeed with ambitious founders like you.

We are looking for new applications to add as our initial Sparks before we open the alpha. If you would like to be one of our first Sparks please email me at dave@sparkmuse.com. I hope you will consider applying!
