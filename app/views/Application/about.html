About

Hi! Thanks for stopping by to find out about Sparkmuse. We are a community of thinkers and doers focused on the early stages of creation- before cofounders, before product, and before users.

What's the Skinny?

Sparkmuse is a community focused on discussing, refining and acting on innovative tech ideas. We welcome any ideas which aim to solve an identifiable need through science, engineering, and technology. This includes "there's an app for that" anythings and alternate energy everythings, from giant man-sized robots to how to type an umlaut, on planet Earth to infinity and beyond. We don't underestimate the power of a good idea.

We especially love ideas where you've got something to show us, even if it's just a sketch. The goal here is not only to critique the idea itself but also to evaluate how your solution will create value. When you post an idea here, we want to find out if it has feet, so you should always be willing to follow through. But don't worry about going it alone, we will be your support network and a place where you can connect with others who want to work with you. So stop watching TV, lets create something together!

Musing at a Glance

Each idea you post gets its own Spark page. You can think of this page as an idea wiki except that discussion is a first-class citizen. This page will allow you to track your progress, explain the problem you are trying to solve and your proposed solution, link to important resources, provide visuals of mockups or other diagrams, and most importantly get the feedback of our community. Sparks are also a connection point where anyone can offer their services and team-up on a project.

Of course, we encourage everyone to particapate in other peoples' ideas that catch their interest. Ideas cross-polinate when given several different points of view. Your contribution to other ideas will both improve that idea and possibly give you a thought for your own that wouldn't occur otherwise.

Sharing and Stealing

Sparkmuse is the beginning of a community in which I've always wanted to participate. "That's a cool idea" has crossed my mind so many times that I've yearned for a place to discuss, vet, and organize those thoughts. Over the years of reading Hacker News, there have often been "Rate My Idea" posts and some who outright said they wish they had a more formal place to share them. Sparkmuse will hopefully become that place.

I know what that little voice inside your head is saying: with sharing comes the possibility of stealing. But who knows what the next big thing will be? Your ideas are fundamentally your own, made in your image. Only you have the vision and only your execution will make it successful. No imposter will ever be able to steal its core, just impersonate its facade. So share, get your idea out in the wild as soon as possible and go to pivot town on it. I promise it will be for the better.
