About Us

Hi! Thanks for stopping by to find out more about Sparkmuse. Here's a little bit about what makes us great.

We're early-stage entrepreneurs.

Before cofounders, before products, and before users, we focus on members honing in on one bright idea. We discuss, refine, and encourage each other to act. We also facilitate connections between entrepreneurs looking for partners to help make their ideas become reality.

We're crazy about science and technology.

Our entrepreneurs develop ideas for solutions in the fields of science, engineering and technology. Not only web applications and mobile tech, but also robots, circuit boards, industrial design, and even human genome technology. We're also open to the softer sciences, medicine, and any cool idea in the larger tech space that helps to solve an identifiable need.

We help each other.

It's lonely sometimes starting the next big thing. That's why our community emphasizes connections. Whether you need advice from someone who's gone before or a brand new partner, this is the place to find it. To keep the love going, we encourage every member to help out the others wherever they can. This includes not only honest feedback but also resources, referrals and mentoring. Together, we can make this a great place.

We're an honest bunch.

As a community, we all participate in each other's journeys. To help our ideas become better, we provide helpful, honest feedback about them. We also commit to keeping each others' ideas confidential and respected. Read more about our honesty policy at our Guidelines page.
